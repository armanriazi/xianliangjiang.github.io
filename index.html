Welcome to Xianliang Jiang's Homepage!
Xianliang Jiang is currently a Lecturer at the Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science in Ningbo University (Ningbo, China). He received the BS degree in Computer Science and Technology from USTC in 2009, the MS degree in Computer Application from Ningbo University in 2012, and the PhD degrees in Computer Science and Technology from Zhejiang University in 2016, respectively. He joined in Ningbo University as a faculty member in 2016.

Research Interests

All topics focus on the late-model networking architectures (e.g. 4G/5G, datacenter, Internet-of-Things)
  • Active Queue Management and Scheduling, Network Measurement and Video Streaming
  • Congestion Control based on Software-Defined, Online-learning and Data-driven Methods
